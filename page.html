Where can I stay overnight?

There are plenty of Airbnb's to check out in the Big Bear area. There are also a number of budget-friendly hotels in the area, including the Holiday Inn Resort, the Bear Creek Resort, and the Motel 6. The hotels in the area are a 10-15 minute drive from the venue.

Which airports are closest to the venue?

The Ontario International Airport (ONT) is 58 miles (~1hr 20min drive) from the venue.

The Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) is 117 miles (~2hr 15min drive) from the venue.

When will official invitations be sent out?

When is the RSVP deadline?

Is there a registry?

Yes! We would be happy to accept cash the day of the event, or any item from our registry on the linked Amazon page.

What's the parking situation like at the venue?

The venue has an on-site parking area!

What's the weather going to be like?

The temperature in Big Bear ranges from 40°F to 70°F during October. It doesn't tend to rain or snow, but please come prepared with layers, especially as it does feel chillier at night.

Is there a dress code for the wedding?

We recommend semi-formal/cocktail attire for the occasion. Just make sure to have comfy jackets/other layers prepared in case of cold or precipitation!

What is there to do in Big Bear?

There's lots to do in Big Bear! You can fish at a variety of places including Boulder Bay, take a hike at any one of its gorgeous trails, have a stroll along the Village to shop for souvenirs and have a bite to eat, or even go to Oktoberfest at Wyatt's Grill & Saloon!
